Quality Services

Expert Sofa Repair Solutions

At Prime Sofa we offer a comprehensive range of sofa repair and restoration services tailored to meet your needs. Whether its a minor repair or a complete makeover, we handle every project with precision and care.

Upholstery Repair and Replacement

Revitalize your sofa with high-quality fabric or leather options. Repair or replace torn, faded, or stained upholstery to match your style.

Cushion Refilling and Replacement

Restore the comfort of your sofa with new foam or fiber fillings. Customize cushion firmness for optimal seating comfort.

Frame Repair and Reinforcement

Fix broken or squeaky frames to ensure structural stability. Reinforce wooden or metal frames for long-lasting durability.

Spring and Webbing Repair

Replace sagging springs and worn-out webbing for better support. Enhance the seating experience with premium materials.

Stitching and Zipper Repairs

Fix loose stitches and replace damaged zippers with precision. Ensure a seamless and neat finish for your sofa.

Customized Sofa Makeovers

Personalize your sofa with tailored designs, colors, and patterns. Transform your old sofa into a modern masterpiece.

Complete Structural Repair for Bedrooms

Over time, your bedrooms may face issues like cracks in walls, floor damage, or roof leaks. Our experienced contractors inspect every corner to identify structural concerns.

Bed Repair and Restoration Services

We handle all types of bed frames, including wooden, metal, and adjustable models, and provide quick, efficient solutions to extend the life of your bed.

Bed Frame Repair and Maintenance

A damaged bed frame can affect the comfort and stability of your sleeping area. Whether your bed has a broken leg, loose slats, or a squeaky frame, our experts are here to fix it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sofa repair take?

The timeline depends on the extent of the repair. Minor fixes can take a day, while more extensive work, like upholstery replacement, may take a few days. Well provide an estimated completion time during the consultation.

Can you match the original fabric or color of my sofa?

Yes, we offer a wide range of fabric and color options to match or complement your sofas original look. Our team will guide you in selecting the best match.

Do you repair leather sofas?

Absolutely! We specialize in leather repairs, including patching, recoloring, and restoring damaged or faded leather surfaces.
